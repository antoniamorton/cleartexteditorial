About Me

Your Plain Language specialist

I’m Antonia Morton, and I’ve spent the last 25 years editing (and sometimes writing)
a diverse range of material:

  • academic papers, theses, dissertations
  • novels and stories
  • non-fiction books
  • reports and publications
  • educational and training material
  • PowerPoint presentations and speeches
  • websites and blogs
  • brochures, posters and flyers
  • advertising and marketing copy
  • magazine and newspaper stories
  • manuals, memoirs, signage, CD liners,
    product blurbs, poems...

... and many, many more. If you can write it, I can tidy it up.

I’ve been a passionate fan of words, writing, and stories ever since my bookish childhood in England. In the decades since then, I’ve:

  • acquired degrees in English Literature and Journalism
  • written hundreds of articles for various Canadian magazines and newspapers
  • worked as an editor for almost a quarter century.

I’m located in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, and I welcome English-language clients from all around the world.
