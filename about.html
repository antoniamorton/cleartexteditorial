About/Contact

Antonia Morton

I’ve spent 25 years working on a wide variety of editing projects. These range from novels to government reports, from corporate brochures to personal memoirs, from business websites to magazine articles, from PhD dissertations to non-fiction books, and from organizational publications to blogs.

If you can write it, I can tidy it up.

My clients appreciate the fact that I'm prompt, responsive, and focused on meeting all their editing needs. I specialize in Plain Language, and my life’s work is making text more reader-friendly.

I’ve been a passionate fan of words, writing, and stories ever since my bookish childhood in England. In the decades since then, I’ve acquired degrees in English Literature and Journalism; and I’ve written several hundreds of articles for various Canadian magazines and newspapers.

When I’m not slaving over some problematic piece of copy, I’m usually to be found (depending on the season) swimming in the Ottawa River, kayaking on Meech Lake, cross-country skiing in the Gatineau Park, singing folk songs in pubs with other expat Brits—or just curled up with a good book or film.

View my LinkedIn profile, and my website dedicated to academic editing.

I’m located in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, and I welcome English-language clients from all around the world.
